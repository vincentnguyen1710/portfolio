What I Did

I solo-built this MVP end-to-end — starting in July 2025 and covering every layer of the product lifecycle:

  • Research — Identified the problem space through user interviews and competitive analysis of existing IT helpdesk tools.
  • Design — Designed the full UI/UX, from information architecture and wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes and a lightweight design system.
  • Development — Built and shipped a workable MVP, including the AI chat interface, admin configuration panel, and backend integrations.
